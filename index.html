Hello

I'm Tanner Yuhase,

Web/Design/Development philosopher.
Basically, my professional passion is creating concepts and visual ideas and translating them into a project or strategy. I enjoy working with people to help translate a dream into a functioning extention of their product.

I'm glad to work in a field that brings my love of art, music, logic, and technology together to create wonderful/useful things for others to enjoy.

At Work

I work at West Virginia University doing web development for WVU Healthcare. I work on the intranet mostly, but I get to work with some great people so thats always good. Always looking for opportunities to learn more and grow as a designer (and person).

My focus is creating cohesive projects using modern web techniques.
Accessible HTML5, simple and coherent CSS3, cross-browser compatibility, SEO & marketing strategies, I've worked with JavaScript and JQuery, and large number of Content Managemnt Systems including Wordpress, Kirby, Kentico, Umbraco, and WVU's CleanSlate.

I have a BFA in Art and Design from WVU, which blessed me with a strong art background. Understanding and translating core art philosophies such as information hierarchy, composition, and color theory can make any project better. I thoroughly enjoy the critique process and have never been against starting again from the ground up to achieve the best results.

As with most in my field, I make use of numerous programs and tools such as the adobe suite, visual studio, sublime text, notepad, MS office products, ftp programs, and much more.

At Home

Outside of work, I'm mostly a family man. They take up most of my time and I prefer it that way. I do occasionally manage to play some video games, try to be healthy, or do some other form of nerdy thing. I love music and singing along. I was in school bands for almost a couple decades. I like listening to ideas and thinking about nothing. Here's some of my family!
